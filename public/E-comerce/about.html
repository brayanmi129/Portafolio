ABOUT US:

“THE THRIFT APP”

Is a groundbreaking non-profit platform exclusively designed for exchanging second-hand products, especially clothing. You can post clothes and other articles you no longer wear or use, as well as browse others you are interested in, and, if there are items that catch your eye, it’s possible to swap them among the other users.

MISSION:

Its aim is to encourage people to reuse multiple items to a much greater extent than they usually do and avoid buying unnecessary ones.

VISSION:

It is expected that by this time next year, this service will have significantly decreased fast fashion production and also the platform will have more than 10.000 active users.
